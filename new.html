Blue & Grey COMING

It is an emotional pop ballad genre centered on acoustic guitar sound, and is a song full of the color and charm of V who participated in the lyrics and composition. The lyrics express inner depression and anxiety in blue and gray, bringing out the sympathy of the listeners. The acoustic guitar player takes the center of the song, and the vocal Humming in the second half raises the emotions of the song.

Dynamite NEW $24.99

On August 21, 2020, BTS' first digital single, which was simultaneously released worldwide, was published in this album. The lyrics talk about the preciousness of life and the speciality of life through the moments of small daily life, and contain the wishes of BTS that they want to spread a message of vitality and hope in times when everyone has a hard time with Corona 19.

Origin

Who are we?

Committed to the mission of “Music & Artist for Healing,” comforting and inspiring people around the globe through our music and artist, Big Hit Entertainment continues to make new innovations to the music industry business model. Big Hit strives to become the world's best music-based entertainment lifestyle platform company. Big Hit places our highest priority on global trend-leading “content” and our key customer, “fans”. The company continuously strives to satisfy our customers with high standards and constant improvement. Artists under the Big Hit label include BTS, referred to as “The Beatles of the 21st century”, “global super-rookies” TOMORROW X TOGETHER, and “soul ballad singer” Lee Hyun.
